Mutton Sukka

+ Add to Favourites
Medium 45-60 mins Non-veg

Ingredients

2 cups basmati rice
500 grams mutton (cut pieces)
2 onions, thinly sliced
2 tomatoes, chopped
2 green chillies
2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1/2 cup yogurt
1 tsp red chilli powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes and drain.
  2. Heat oil or ghee and add whole spices. Sauté until aromatic.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until mushy.
  6. Add mutton, green chillies, chilli powder, turmeric and salt.
  7. Mix well and cook until the meat is tender.
