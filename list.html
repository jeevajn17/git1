2 cups basmati rice
500 grams, cut into pieces
2 onions, thinly sliced
2 tomatoes, chopped
2 green chillies, slit
2 tsp ginger-garlic paste
1/2 cup yogurt
1 tsp red chili powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder
1/2 tsp turmeric powder

Instructions:

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice in water for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat oil or ghee and add whole spices. Sauté until aromatic.
  3. Add sliced onions and cook until golden brown.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for a minute.
  5. Add chopped tomatoes and cook until mushy.
